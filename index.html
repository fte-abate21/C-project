Ethiopian Orthodox Church

Welcome

The Ethiopian Orthodox Tewahedo Church is one of the oldest Christian churches in the world. It plays a vital role in the spiritual, cultural, and historical life of the Ethiopian people.

About the Church

The church was established in the 4th century and has preserved ancient traditions of Christianity. It follows ancient liturgy, teaches values of peace and love, and celebrates many unique holidays like Meskel, Timket, and Fasika.

Services

  • Weekly Mass and Holy Communion
  • Confession and Spiritual Guidance
  • Bible Study Programs
  • Church Choir and Youth Fellowship
  • Charity and Community Service
